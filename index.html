A space where ideas and Bootcamp grads unite to build Meaningful Viable Products

Make it MVP is a collaborative community where ideas meet skilled creators to build products. From ideation through development, collaborative communities consisting of founders, developers, and designers will be guided through the lifecycle of product development to establish a meaningful viable product.

FEATURED APPS

Features

Are you a Builder?

During your time at Make It MVP, you will work together in a cross-functional team to build a meaningful viable product. You will contribute to the entire product cycle from conception to launch

Ideate

Before your first sprint, you will meet the founder of your app for a Pitch meeting.
Plan It

Your team will kick off the first sprint with a plan of action and continue each sprint the same way.
Build It

From User Research all the way through Development your team will work together to create a fully testable product.
Pitch It

After four complete sprints, you will present the MVP to the founder

Are you a founder?

From Ideation to pitch, the Make it MVP team will guide you through the entire process. Weekly meetings with your team. Mentor sessions with our staff and professionals. Community-wide workshops for all founders.

Dream It

Tell us the problem you are solving through technology.

Design It

Design your product. Be prepared to defend your vision.
Build It

Engage in the Building process. Define features by importance. See the product come to life!
Launch It

Pitch your idea with your team. Engage in User Testing. Start your fundraising.
